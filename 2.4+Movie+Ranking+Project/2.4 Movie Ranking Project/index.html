The Best Anime according to Meko

My Top 3 Anime of all-time.

Mahoraba ~Heartful Days~

A story about a young man going to Tokyo to attend art school. His mom tells him to stay in Narutaki Inn for his board and lodging while he attends school. His mom tells him that Inn is ran by her sister. When the boy arrives he finds out that his mom's sister daughter runs the inn now as the new landlady. He falls in love with her at first sight, but the landlady is not all as she appears to be.

Oreimo

A story about a brother and sister who at first don't see eye to eye, but one night the brother finds out that his sister is into EROGE, in particular Little Sister EROGE! The sister tells him to keep this a secret and the two end up bounding over EROGE. Oniichan doesn't know his little sister may love him a lot more than he thinks...

Zero no Tsukiama

A story about a failed witch who can only do explosion magic. During a summoning ceremony, she summons a human boy to be her familiar. She ends up falling head over heals for the boy, and in doing so becomes stronger and realizes her self worth as a witch.
